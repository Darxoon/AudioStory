Controls

  • Navigate through menus with W and S. Press Space to talk to a person or to fight an enemy.
  • Press Space while talking to a person to skip their text
  • In fights, the enemy will either make a left panned, right panned or centered noise. The player then has to dodge in that direction with A, W or D with perfect timing. Depending on how well it was timed, the more damage the player makes. If the dodge has failed, you loose health.
  • Press Esc to open the pause menu. There, press S to save and Q to quit. Press Esc again to resume.
